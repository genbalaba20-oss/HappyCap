About HappyCap

More Than
a Cap.

HappyCap was born in 2026 from a simple belief: every Filipino deserves access to quality headwear without breaking the bank. What started as a one-person operation in Cebu has grown into a brand trusted by hundreds of customers nationwide.

We source from certified suppliers across the Philippines and work directly with manufacturers to keep our prices honest and our quality uncompromising.

Meet the Team

The Founders

Founder & Chairman
Genelyn Balaba

Genelyn founded HappyCap in 2026 with a mission to make premium caps accessible to every Filipino. A Business Administration graduate of Asian College of Technology, he spent two years in fashion retail before launching the brand from his family's garage in Cebu City at just 25 years old.

"I started HappyCap because I believe every Filipino deserves access to quality headwear without breaking the bank."

Chief Executive Officer
Caryl Ann Anciano

Caryl joined HappyCap in 2022 as COO and became CEO in 2024. With 10+ years in retail management and e-commerce, she has scaled the company's operations nationwide, expanding to 15 store locations and launching the sustainable cap line.

"Our success comes from putting our customers and team members first. When you take care of people, the business takes care of itself."
